Mission Statement

January 20, 2025

Paramount Crypto strives to become a universal cryptocurrency used for online gaming across multiple platforms. To achieve this, the vision has been broken down into 5 phases.

Phase I

During Phase I, the following will be implemented: (1 Month - Completion date: 01 MAR 2025)

                - Concept Creation

                - Website Creation

                - Mission Statement Creation

                 During "Phase I", the major point of focus will be Concept creation and website creation. The website will be hosted via GitHub. The website itself will consist of a Mission statement page, a Current Status" page and contact information. The mission statement will consist of a clear guideline of that the "Paramount Crypto" project is and expected timelines.

Phase II

During Phase II, the following will be implemented: (1-2 months - Completion date: 01 MAY 2025)

                - Creation of Assets

                - Asset Distribution

                - Market Placement

                 "Phase II" consists of Creating Assets, Asset Distribution and Market Placement. The creation of the crypto coin will occur during this stage. The coin will consist of 1 Billion tokens. Out of the coins created, 250,000,000 will be retained for future acquisition of assets. The coin will then be placed in several markets and be available to investors and supporters for purchase.

Phase III

Phase III, the following will be implemented: (6-12 months - Completion date :01 NOV 2025 -to- 01 MAY 2026)

                - Promotion and Expansion

                 During "Phase III", the major focus will be engagement with potential investors and expansion into bigger markets. Once the desired price cap is reached, regardless of time frame, the project will move into "Phase IV". On the contrary, "Phase III" could be extended in order to ensure an adequate amount of capital is obtained when moving into the next phase.

Phase IV

During Phase IV, the following will be implemented: (6 months - Completion date: 01 NOV 2026)

                - Sell off of Assets

                - Create Gaming Studio

                - Develop Video Game

                 "Phase IV"- This phase will happen once PMT Crypto coin has reached an acceptable amount to facilitate this stage. The majority of the retained PMT coin will be sold to fun the project. Once the assets are sold, the creation of the PMT Crypto Game Studio will begin. Local developers will be used to develop a game studio website as well as begin the creation of video games that will only be available through the game studio website, purchasable with PMT coin.

Phase V

During Phase V, the following will be implemented: (indefinitely)

                - Retained Assets/Partnerships

                 "Phase V" will consist of the contacting video game studios and developing partnerships for games to be exclusively purchased with the PMT Crypto Coin. This will be done either through the PMT game website or through partnerships with virtual game platforms. This will be done by offering each company contacted and partnered with, an amount, to be determined, in PMT token for exclusive games where PMT is used for both game and game content purchases.

All remaining assets will be applied to the overall maintenance of the platform and help implementation across multiple platforms and markets.
